Flinkhub - University groups built on telegram OS.

FlinkhubSocial
Teen
148

Flinkhub lets you join interest or location based groups where you can meet and interact with your university alumni with same interests or career trajectory.

You can search for and discover groups like Data Science, Software development, Finance, Traveling, Entrepreneurs & VCs, Fitness Enthusiast, etc or you can connect with you alumni from the same batch or same location.

Interacting with the similar minded people will help you increase your knowledge and help you in your career. Alumni can also connect you to companies for a job referral or to an investor for your startup funding.

Flinkhub is based on telegram and is an open source project which is built with a focus on speed and security.

Features:
* Chat with special interest groups alumni from your university
* See profiles of the people in the group
* Identify and discover interest groups
* Find alumni and friends in your location
* Connect with your batchmates

Permissions Requested are primarily from telegram since Flinkhub is built on top of it:
* Contacts
* Camera
* Location
* Phone

Flinkhub is currently live in Manipal University, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur), BITS Pilani, Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIM Bangalore), Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IIT Delhi). You can also create for your own university as shown during the onboarding process.
