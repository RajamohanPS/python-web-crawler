19 Best Actor Website Templates 2020 ( HTML & WordPress)

We proudly present you with a collection of the best actor website templates that you can currently get your hands on. These will immediately raise your potential with solid and business-driven web design.

In a world where technology greatly influences people’s lives, artists, professionals, and creative individuals should have a good website to promote and market self. If you’re just starting out your career you can enhance a sense of professionalism with an online presence. So, don’t lose out on opportunities, make an investment in your career with a website that will clearly represent who you are, what you do and why they should have you in their business. In this list of actor website templates, you can create useful and mobile-friendly websites.

If you’re an actor, it’s best to create a website that will show up once potential employers look for you on the web. It must showcase important things about you, exhibit your works, video clips and how to contact you. We have collected this list of actor website templates to help you build an awesome acting career website. Having this collection, you can create a simple static HTML website or a WordPress-driven site with amazing design and functionality.

Check out this list, soar high and open up greater opportunities for yourself! With these templates, you can create a professional, useful, easy to navigate and user-friendly actor website.

Actor Website Templates Using WordPress

Here’s a list of premium WordPress themes for actors, artists, models, and other professionals. All of these themes allow you to build pages without coding skills required. These themes integrate powerful plugins to boosts the theme’s performance.

Burbank

A mobile-friendly website is a must-have if you wish to be in the competition. Here’s Burbank, one of the best actor website templates that you can grab for your actor website. It has a clean, minimalist design that has the most essential elements you need to create an actor’s website. From social integration, video clips or reel, biography, set of photographs, latest news, etc., Burbank is ready to provide you with such elements.

This theme has a powerful admin that allows you to set your own elements in place – color schemes, typography, layout, fonts, etc. Burbank also integrates the WooCommerce plugin so you can easily transform your site into an eCommerce. Since Burbank is developed with the latest coding standards and is compatible with Yoast SEO plugin, you can expect to have a search engine-friendly website.

Actors

A website that has a simple, clear and easy-to-use navigation is sure to stand out. Here’s Actors WordPress theme, one of the awesome actor website templates that fit best for actors management, model castings, fashion models agencies, and similar websites. Actors has a simple, straightforward design with an easy-to-navigate menu. This theme uses a comp card or zed card for marketing actors/models. Actors also uses talent shortlist so employers can narrow down their search effectively.

Actors integrates the Elementor Page Builder for creating pages easily and quickly. It’s a live page builder that offers no limit of design to achieve your goal. This theme also implements Paid Membership Pro for managing free and paying members. With such plugin, you can easily put restrictions on contents for different levels of users.

Entertainer

Video clips play an essential role in an actors website. Here’s Entertainer, a responsive WordPress theme for comedians, actors, musicians and other professionals. The features of this theme suit well to be part of these actor website templates collection. Furthermore, Entertainer features a minimalist design for displaying a headshot or promotional photo. With this theme, you can display video collection, showcase podcasts, display upcoming shows or tour dates with ease.

In order for the theme to function well, numerous WordPress plugins are integrated. These include WooCommerce for selling anything within your website, Events Calendar Pro, Easy Digital Downloads, Gravity Forms and Jetpack plugins to provide an extra boost of power to your website. Since the responsive site is a must-have, Entertainer ensures that you will have a mobile-first and responsive website almost effortlessly!

In building your site, make sure it’s mobile friendly so you’re not left behind. Formota is here to help you build your credibility online. It contains 12 useful and ready-to-use demos, 4 blog pages, 6 premade headers, and 4 premade filters for movie studios, filmmakers, actors and more. All of these pre-built sites can be imported with one click demo installation. Likewise, this theme is fully responsive so you have a highly mobile-first website when you pick this theme.

In addition, this actor website template is built with HTML5, CSS3 and Bootstrap framework. Thus, regardless of the device used, your site will look stunning and seamless. As it integrates the Visual Composer plugin, Formota allows you to set elements easily using drag and drop. Moreover, the theme is built with WooCommerce compatibility that lets you create an online store with ease in no time! It also uses smooth animation for a more visually appealing display of contents.

This theme can be installed without hassle, hence, you can focus on the content of your website and not on coding or difficult installation process. As it is built WooCommerce ready, you can easily implement an online store on your agency site.
