    I'm Harshit Kashiv. I have 6 years of Product Management experience across companies like Dream11, MPL, Paytm. I have taken over 300 interviews of PMs, APMs and Product Analysts. I'm passionate about guiding aspirants to crack product roles and become good product managers.

    - In-depth understanding of Product Management concepts

    - Build wireframes and mock ups for new product ideas

    No pre-requisites. If you have prior experience in Software, Marketing, Analytics or Design, it's an added advantage.
