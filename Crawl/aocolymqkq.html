How to design & conduct experiments while building products

I’m Atul Anand. I'm a Product Manager who's worked across several startups.

Experimentation is a very important aspect of a Product Manager's job. A good PM conducts a lot of creative and measured experiments to arrive at a product that fits the market need.

In this live workshop, I'll show you how to design and conduct experiments while building products. We'll work on a real problem statement and go through the journey of creating experiments, measuring outcomes and fine-tuning based on the outcomes.

  • Get hands-on experience of designing a product experiment
  • Insight into the role of a Product Manager
